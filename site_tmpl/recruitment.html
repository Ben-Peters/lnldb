Hi!

We are Lens and Lights, a group of students with a shared passion for event production. We amplify our technical experience and leadership skills by working in teams, with clients, and with professional grade equipment. Throughout the year we support countless events with quality lighting, sound, and projection services.

Lighting

LNL provides lighting for a variety of events with our inventory of professional grade equipment. We illuminate events including simple lighting on a stage, full on dance parties, stage performances, live bands, receptions, outdoor area lighting, and large robotics competitions! The equipment we own includes a variety of conventional, LED, and Arc fixtures allowing our members tons of creativity! Highlights include several pairs of moving head spot fixtures, many moving head LED wash fixtures, wireless uplights, and over 100 conventional Source Four fixtures. We also have a variety of lighting consoles to control them all ranging from small analog consoles to large and complex consoles in the EOS and HOG families. Now if all that sounds like a bunch of jibberish, please come help with one of our events and we will be happy to explain everything from the basics through the advanced. We also encourage you to checkout our workshops!

Sound

Lens and Lights does many different sound events on campus! They range from smaller events like providing a wireless microphone for a speaker, to a Capella shows, to music on the quad, to full blown robotics competitions like BattleCry at WPI. We have several different speakers, mics, consoles and more we use to have the best event possible! If you've ever been interested in how sound gets from something like your phone out to big speakers, feel free to stop by a sound event and we will show you the ropes (...and the cables)!
